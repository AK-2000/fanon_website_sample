What is Fanon?

Fanon is the most engaging platform for visual fanfiction. Fanfiction refers to a genre of stories about popular fictional characters written by fans (Eg. What if Harry Potter was in Slytherin? What if Tony Stark adopted Peter Parker? What if Daenerys Targaryen became the queen of the seven kingdoms?) Fanon enables fanfiction writers to leverage AI and convert their written stories into multi-modal media (comics, video, audio), and to distribute their content. Going beyond content, Fanon will be the home for fans online. The long-term vision is to empower everyone to tell visual stories.
